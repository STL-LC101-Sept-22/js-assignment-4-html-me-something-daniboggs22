Featured Products

Hair Bows

My absolute favorite thing to make is hair bows.Whether you need them for your kids, your family members, a special event, or just need it for yourself, I got you! I make bows for holidays, seasonal, beauty pageants, birthdays, and every other day of the year!

Animal Bows

Do you have a fur baby? I make bows for them too! I make bows to go on the collars of your favorite furry companions! I bet you're probably wondering if I can personalize the bow with their name... the answer is YES!

Lanyards

Need something besides a bland lanyard for your keys? I can help! I've made a ton of custom lanyards including prints from: Hocus Pocus, Stranger Things, TMNT, Halloween Horror Characters, Princesses, 90's inspired, as well as solid colors!

Book Marks

Love reading? Me too! Still using a scrap of paper you found on the coffee table to hold your spot? ...Don't act like you've never done it! I was too, until I started making these adorable bookmarks and magnetic page markers!

Personalized Apparel

Need a shirt to support your favorite sports team? How about a shirt with your favorite quote on it? Maybe a birthday or holiday shirt? Let me design one for you!

Custom Orders

Need an item that you just can't find online? Or maybe you've found something, but want something slightly different? Chances are, I can help! Some custom orders I have made include: Specific style or character bows, Beauty Pageant bows, shirts, Bow holders, Cheerleading style bows, personalized bookmarks, personalized lanyards... the list goes on and on! I have learned how to make so many different things because of customers asking for a custom order!

About Me

I began making bows for my two adorable girls. It turns out, I had so much fun making them, I made too many for them to even be able to wear! I decided to reach out to my friends and see who may be interested in purchasing bows (and funding my new found hobby!). I started making bows for my friends, and then their friends! After that I branched out and started making all sorts of fun things!
